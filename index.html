Services

We provide tutoring to any DCPS student who wants it, through any online video-chat platform, such as Skype, Google Hangouts, or Zoom. You can register for tutoring on the Book With Us page. If you need help using video-chatting platforms feel free to email or call us for instructions.

We tutor:

  • Math (from elementary to AP Calculus BC)
  • Programming and Computer Science (up to AP Computer Science, as well as Python and C++)
  • Science (from elementary to AP Physics, Biology, and Chemistry)
  • History and Social Studies (from elementary to AP World History, U.S. History, and U.S. Government)
  • English (from elementary to AP English Language, English Literature)
  • Languages (Spanish, Mandarin Chinese, and Hindi)

We also offer enrichment clubs every week in an effort to encourage extracurricular growth. As of now we offer Book Club, Bullet Journaling Club, and a Math Club. We will add further offerings as demand grows. Feel free to contact us with any suggestions you may have!
